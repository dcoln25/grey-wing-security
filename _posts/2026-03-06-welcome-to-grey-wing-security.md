Welcome to the Grey Wing Security blog. This is where we'll share insights, analysis, and practical guidance on cybersecurity topics that matter to businesses and security professionals alike.

## What to Expect

We plan to cover a wide range of topics, including:

- **Threat Landscape Updates** -- Breaking down the latest threats and what they mean for your organization.
- **Incident Response Lessons** -- Real-world takeaways from the front lines of cyber defense.
- **Compliance Deep Dives** -- Practical guidance on navigating frameworks like NIST, SOC 2, and HIPAA.
- **Security Best Practices** -- Actionable tips you can implement today to strengthen your posture.
- **Industry News and Commentary** -- Our perspective on major developments in the cybersecurity world.

## Why We Write

We believe that sharing knowledge makes everyone more secure. The threat landscape moves fast, and staying informed is one of the best defenses an organization can have.

Whether you're a CISO, IT manager, or business owner trying to understand your risk, we aim to make our content accessible and useful.
