## Managed Security Services

We act as an extension of your team, providing continuous monitoring, threat detection, and security operations management. Our managed services include:

- Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)
- 24/7 threat monitoring and alerting
- Vulnerability management and patching guidance
- Endpoint detection and response (EDR)
- Regular security reporting and executive briefings

---

## Incident Response

When a security incident strikes, every minute counts. Our incident response team provides:

- Rapid triage and containment
- Digital forensics and root cause analysis
- Malware analysis and threat intelligence
- Recovery planning and execution
- Post-incident review and hardening recommendations

---

## Compliance

Navigating regulatory and industry compliance can be complex. We help you achieve and maintain compliance with:

- NIST Cybersecurity Framework
- SOC 2 Type I and Type II
- HIPAA Security Rule
- PCI DSS
- State and federal privacy regulations
