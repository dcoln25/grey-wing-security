## Managed Security Services

**Your SOC team — without the six-figure headcount.**

Most growing companies know they need 24/7 security coverage but can't justify building an in-house SOC. We plug in as your dedicated security operations team — monitoring, detecting, and responding so your engineers can ship product instead of chasing alerts.

### What this looks like in practice

- SIEM deployment and tuning (Splunk, Sentinel, Elastic, or your stack of choice)
- 24/7 threat monitoring with real-time alerting and triage
- Endpoint detection and response (EDR) management — CrowdStrike, SentinelOne, Defender
- Vulnerability scanning, prioritization, and patching guidance
- Monthly security briefings your leadership team will actually read

*We don't just send alerts. We investigate, contextualize, and tell you exactly what to do.*

## Incident Response

**When something breaks, every minute matters.**

A breach doesn't wait for business hours. Our incident response team mobilizes fast — containing threats, preserving evidence, and getting your systems back online while keeping legal and executive teams informed.

### How we respond

- Rapid triage and threat containment
- Digital forensics and root cause analysis
- Malware reverse engineering and indicator extraction
- Recovery planning and system hardening post-incident
- Post-incident review with actionable recommendations

*Whether it's ransomware at 2 AM or suspicious access from somewhere you don't operate in — we've seen it and know how to handle it.*

## Identity & Access Management (IAM)

**Migrating IdPs is painful. We make it painless.**

Switching from Okta to OneLogin? Consolidating Azure AD tenants after an acquisition? Replacing a legacy LDAP setup? Identity provider migrations are high-risk and disruptive projects — and we've done them many times.

### We help with

- IdP migration planning and execution — Okta, OneLogin, Azure AD (Entra ID), Ping Identity, JumpCloud, Google Workspace
- SSO and MFA rollout across SaaS tools (SAML, OIDC, SCIM provisioning)
- Zero Trust access policy design — least privilege, conditional access, device trust
- Directory consolidation and user lifecycle automation
- Access reviews, RBAC, and entitlement cleanup
- HRIS integration (BambooHR, Workday, Rippling) for onboarding/offboarding automation

*Your identity layer is your security perimeter. We'll make sure it operates like one.*
